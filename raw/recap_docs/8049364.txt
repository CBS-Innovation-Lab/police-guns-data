              GOVERNMENT'S UNOPPOSED MOTION TO DISMISS

     Pursuant to Fed. R. Cr. P. 48(a), the United States of

America, without objection from the defendant, moves the Court to

dismiss Count One of the Indictment with prejudice as it applies

to Defendant Alan DeJesus-Quinones.         The defendant pled guilty to

state charges arising from the incident underlying the charges in

this case.    As a result of his plea, the defendant was sentenced

to 10 years imprisonment, with two years to serve.               Accordingly,

the government respectfully requests that the Court dismiss Count

One of the Indictment as it relates to Defendant Alan DeJesus-

Quinones.
