STEARNS, D.J.

Petitioner Freddy Torres, Jr., proceeding pro se, brings this petition to modify
his sentence pursuant to 18 U.S.C. § 3582(c)(2). The petition is DENIED. As the
Government points out, in exchange for considerable sentencing benefits, Torres
waived any right to appeal his conviction or sentence or to bring a collateral
challenge under either 28 U.S.C. § 2255 or 18 U.S.C. § 3582(c)(2). Having reaped
the benefit of his bargain, Torres Will be kept to his Word.l

The petition is DISMSSED With prejudice Petitioner is advised that any
request for the issuance of a Certiticate of Appealability pursuant to 28 U.S.C. § 2253
of the court’s Order issued November 22, 2011, is DENIED, the court seeing no

meritorious or substantial basis supporting an appeal.

 

1 In any event, Torres is ineligible for consideration under the “crack” cocaine
amendments to the Sentencing Guidelines. See Dillon v. United States, 130 S.Ct.
2683 (2010).

 

 

The Clerk may now close the case.
SO ORDERED.
/s/ Richard G. Stearns
