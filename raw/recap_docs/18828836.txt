                                         ORDER

       Having considered the Report and Recommendation filed on January 24, 2013
(docket entry 47) on a Rule 11 proceeding of defendant held before U.S. Magistrate Judge
Camille L. Vélez-Rivé on January 18, 2013, to which no opposition has been filed, the same
is APPROVED. Accordingly, the plea of guilty of defendant is accepted. The Court FINDS
that her plea was voluntary and intelligently entered with awareness of her rights and the
consequences of pleading guilty and contains all elements of the offense charged in the
indictment.
       This case was referred to the U.S. Probation Office for preparation of a Presentence
Investigation Report since January 18, 2013. The sentencing hearing is set for April 23,
2013 at 4:30 PM.
       SO ORDERED.
       At San Juan, Puerto Rico, on February 15, 2013.
