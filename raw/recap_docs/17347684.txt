GERTNER, D.J.,

WHEREAS, on August 22, 2007 a federal grand jury sitting in
the District of Massachusetts returned a one-count Indictment
charging defendant Christopher Jones (the “Defendant”) with being
a Felon in Possession of a Firearm and Ammunitionr in violation
of 18 U.S.C. § 922(§)(1);

wHEREAS, the Forfeiture Allegation of the Indictment sought
the forfeiture, upon conviction of the offense alleged in Count
One of the Indictment, of the firearm and ammunition involved in
the commission of the offense, pursuant to 18 U.S.C. § 924(d) and
28 U.S.C. § 2461(€), including without limitation a Colt .45
caliber semi-automatic handgun bearing serial number DA09346 (the
“Handgun”) and containing 6 rounds of .45 caliber ammunition (the
“Ammunition”);

wHEREAS, the Indictment further provided that, if the
Handgun and Ammunition (collectively, the “Property”), as a

located upon the exercise of due diligence; (b) has been
