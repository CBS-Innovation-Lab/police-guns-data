                                         ORDER

       Having considered the Report and Recommendation filed on November 16, 2012
(docket entry 29) on a Rule 11 proceeding of defendant José Miguel Serrano-Ares held
before U.S. Magistrate Judge Silvia Carreño-Coll on November 1, 2012, to which no
objection has been filed, the same is APPROVED. Accordingly, the plea of guilty of
defendant is accepted. The Court FINDS that his plea was voluntary and intelligently
entered with awareness of his rights and the consequences of pleading guilty and contains
all elements of the offense charged in the indictment.
       This case was referred to the U.S. Probation Office for preparation of a Presentence
Investigation Report since November 1, 2012.        The sentencing hearing is set for
February 5, 2013 at 4:30 PM.
       SO ORDERED.
       At San Juan, Puerto Rico, on December 6, 2012.
