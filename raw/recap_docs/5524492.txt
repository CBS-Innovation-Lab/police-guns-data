DISMISSAL OF INDICTMEHI AGAINST MARK D. HATTHEWS

Pursuant to FRCP 48(a), the Acting United States Attorney for
the District of Massachusetts, Diane C. Freniere, hereby dismisses
the indictment against defendant MARK D. MATTHEWS, which charges
him with possession of a firearm by a felon, in violation of 18
U.S.C. § 922(g)(1). In support of this dismissal, the government
states that in light of this Court's order dated March l, 2006,
suppressing certain evidence, the government will be unable to
prove the charge in the Indictment beyond a reasonable doubt.
